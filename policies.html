Shipping Policy

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50. Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Delivery typically takes 3-7 business days depending on your location.

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days (Free over $50)
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days ($15)
  • International: Calculated at checkout (7-14 business days)

Returns & Refunds

If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return unused products within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Return shipping is free for exchanges.

  • Items must be unused and in original packaging.
  • Contact support@kaibe.com to initiate a return.
  • Refunds processed within 5-7 business days.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. We only collect information necessary to process orders and improve your experience. We never sell or share your data with third parties.

Terms of Service
